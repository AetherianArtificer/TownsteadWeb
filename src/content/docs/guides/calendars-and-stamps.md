Townstead calendars control how dates are shown in your world. A pack can define month names, year numbering, and other calendar details so the world feels like its own place.

## Calendar Dates

The calendar is the date you see in game. It can be adjusted for world setup, storytelling, or compatibility with a pack's setting.

Changing the displayed date does not automatically age villagers. Townstead separates calendar display from the life-day counter used for aging.

## Life Days

Life stages use Townstead life days for biological and narrative pacing. Life days are covered in more detail in [Life Stages](/guides/life-stages/).

## Stamps

Stamps record important dates for people, villages, and events. More documentation is coming for the full stamp system.
